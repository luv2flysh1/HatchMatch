HATCHMATCH - APP OVERVIEW
==================================

A mobile app (iPhone & Android) that tells you which flies are working
best on any river or lake.

SCOPE: US waters for MVP, global expansion in later phase
SPECIES: All species supported (trout recommendations strongest initially)


WHAT IT DOES
------------

* Search for any river or lake by name or find waters near you
* Get AI-powered fly recommendations based on season, weather,
  hatch charts, and water conditions
* Plan single or multi-destination fishing trips
* Find fly shops near your destination with driving directions
* Buy recommended flies from online retailers
* Log your catches to help improve recommendations for everyone


KEY FEATURES (MVP)
------------------

1. WATER BODY SEARCH
   - Search by name and location (city, state)
   - Find rivers and lakes near your current location
   - Save favorite waters

2. FLY RECOMMENDATIONS
   - Top flies for any water body
   - Based on: season, weather, hatch charts, water type, species
   - Includes fly name, size, and technique tips

3. TRIP PLANNING
   - Single day trips or multi-day trips
   - Single location or multiple waters
   - Auto-updates recommendations when you open the app
   - Save multiple future trips

4. FLY SHOP FINDER
   - Shops near any water body
   - Address, phone, hours
   - One-tap directions in your favorite maps app

5. CATCH REPORTING (Phase 2)
   - Log what flies worked for you
   - See what's working for other anglers
   - Community-driven data improves recommendations


HOW FLY RECOMMENDATIONS WORK
----------------------------

Data comes from multiple sources:

1. AI predictions using seasonal hatch charts, weather data,
   and water conditions

2. User-reported catches from the angler community

3. Aggregated fishing reports from guides and shops

4. Future: Direct partnerships with local fly shops
   for real-time "what's hot" updates


BUSINESS MODEL
--------------

Freemium - free to use with optional premium upgrade.

PRICING:
- Free: Core features with limits
- Premium: $4.99/month or $29.99/year (50% discount)
- Season Pass: $19.99 for Apr-Oct (alternative option)

FREE vs PREMIUM:

  Feature                  Free              Premium
  -------                  ----              -------
  Fly recommendations      1 water/day       Unlimited
  Trip planning            1 active trip     Unlimited
  Fly shop locator         Full access       Full access
  Catch reporting          Full access       Full access
  Offline mode             No                Yes
  Auto-refresh trips       No                Yes
  Ad-free                  No                Yes

Additional revenue from affiliate commissions when users buy flies
through retailer links in the app.


API COSTS (Claude AI for Recommendations)
-----------------------------------------

Estimated cost per fly recommendation request: ~$0.004 - $0.005
(less than half a cent using Claude Haiku model)

Monthly cost projections:
- 10,000 requests/month:  ~$40-50
- 50,000 requests/month:  ~$200-250
- 100,000 requests/month: ~$400-500

Cost optimization:
- Aggressive caching (same water + date = cached result)
- Batch requests for multi-water trips
- Smart cache expiry (12-24 hours)


TECHNOLOGY
----------

- Built with Expo (React Native) for iOS and Android
- Single codebase, native performance on both platforms
- Works offline for remote fishing locations (Premium)
